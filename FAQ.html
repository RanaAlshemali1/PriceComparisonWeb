Can I save time shopping online?
Yes, you’ll save the time and effort. Our website combine three of the major retail stores. You’ll have the option to search them all or one of them.


Can I save money when I shop on the Web?
By using our website, you’ll have the ability of explore the different prices and chose what meets you budget.


Can I learn enough about the product I want to buy?

Yes, you can easily click on the product’s name to the original website and see more details and what is the customers’ opinion of the product.


Can I just research products online, and then go to a local store?
Yes. Actually this is one of the main agenda in creating this website. To save time and Money!


What Stores?
As of now, we have three major stores which are Walmart, Target and Best Buy.


Where Do I Find a Store?
After choosing a product, you can go the original website and enter your zip code, then find the nearest one to you. Or using google map.
Adding this feature to our website is one of the future plans for this website.


How Do I Find a Product?
Simply, you click on the “Compare Price” tap. Then, type the product’s name that you wish to search. After that click “Select All” to select all stores, or choose one of them if preferred. Finally, hit the search button.


Why Register?
By registering, you will have the ability to add products to your wish list to compare prices or just as a list to remind you of what to buy. All product will be saved for you when you come back.


How do I get Help?
If you encounter any problem, please contact us. We’ll be more than happy to assist you. To contact us click on the “Contact Us” tab in the menu. Then fill in the form and click on send.


How Do I Order?
You can go to the original website to place an order. Or by visiting the nearest retail store.


When will I get my Package? And what about Returns?
Our website is only dedicated to search prices to compare them. After that, the website is not responsible about anything. Each store has its own policy.
