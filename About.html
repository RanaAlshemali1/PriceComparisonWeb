About Us

Nowadays, it is hard to decide from which store to buy a new product such as TV or Smartphone. The competition between retail and online stores to get the lowest price makes the decision even harder. It is a time consuming to check all the available stores. Wal-Mart, Target, Best Buy, Sears, Amazon, eBay and many more. This problem motivated us to come up with the idea of creating a website that would compare stores’ prices. Mez-PriceComparison.com is a website that compares the product’s prices among several stores. It saves time, effort and money! Also, on the home page you can find deals and suggested products to help you explore.
